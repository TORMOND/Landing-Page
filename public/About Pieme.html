About Pieme

Pieme is the first of its kind social platform that enables individuals to turn their home into part-time restaurant or event venues.

Just like Uber decentralized the taxi business, Airbnb decentralized the hotel industry, Pieme is decentralizing the restaurant business by enabling individuals to monetize home-cooked meals.

With Pieme, any one can turn their home into a part-time restaurant or event venue and share authentic local dishes with the community around them.
